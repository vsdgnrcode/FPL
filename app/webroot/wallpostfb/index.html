  • How to play

    Choose 11 players from any team to play on any particular day.
    As the players score in the real match, your scores will also increase.
    It is as simple as that. So choose your player carefully and get a chance to win some great rewards.
    User can also take other small challenges in the application and win points for the same. These points shall be accumulated and later can be used as virtual cash in the next version of the game.
    To play the game, Click Here

    Rules:

    Rule 1: Only 11 changes are allowed in a particular day.
    Rule 2: There can be a maximum of 7 players of the same kind (max 7 batsmen or 7 bowlers).
    Rule 3: Teams cannot be changed during the match hours.
    Rule 4: Same player cannot be selected more than once in any particular day.
    Rule 5: Same player cannot be selected more than once in any particular day.

    Group Play

    Create a group, invite your friends to play along with you. Challenge your friends to top the group and have a mini league for yourself.
    If you are in corporate, create a team and challenge your team mates and get to know your team mates better.
    You shall not only show off among your friends, but your group may also get a chance to win a reward of 1 lakh. The group average score will be considered.
    Group average score is calculated as total of each players in the group divided by the total number of people in the group.
